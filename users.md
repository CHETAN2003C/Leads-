## Feature Explanations

### Accessible Modules
- **Dashboard**: High-level sales metrics, total opportunities, predicted revenue.
- **CSV Upload**: Interface to upload new leads and activity streams.
- **Top Opportunities**: Leads prioritized by AI intent classification.
- **AI Copilot**: Automated talking points and outreach generator.
- **User Management**: Verification system and role changer for administrators.
